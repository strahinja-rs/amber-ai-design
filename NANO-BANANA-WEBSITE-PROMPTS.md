# Iteration Tips

**For all prompts:**
- "Use exactly the pendant from my reference image"
- "The glow should only be at the edge/rim, soft amber, not throughout"
- "Maintain warm color palette — no cool tones"
- "Luxury jewelry aesthetic, not tech gadget"

**If pendant doesn't match:**
- "Keep the pendant design exactly as shown: cream ceramic face, rose gold bezel"
- "The pendant is approximately 38mm x 46mm, asymmetric pebble shape"

**For lifestyle shots:**
- "Natural, documentary style — not staged or stock photo"
- "Shallow depth of field, pendant in focus"
- "Golden hour / warm ambient lighting"
